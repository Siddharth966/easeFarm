Product Details

COOPEX Insecticidal Dust is a widely used insecticide powder that contains Permethrin as its active ingredient. It's effective for the control of various insects, including termites and other pests.
COOPEX Insecticidal Dust can be applied in areas where termites or pests are commonly found. Its powder form allows easy penetration into cracks, crevices, and hard-to-reach areas where insects nest or hide.
Permethrin, the active ingredient, is a synthetic chemical that belongs to the pyrethroid family. It works by disrupting the nervous system of insects, leading to paralysis and death.
